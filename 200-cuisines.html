200 Cuisines

Ascending the Seven Summits of Savor

Digital, 2016 - today (ongoing)

How would a simple daily task, eating, manifest itself if the we sought parallels from contemporary, TED talk-like narratives? In 200 Cuisines: something for your bucket list we became interested in how everyday activities become gamified and professionalized. Inspired by the hacks of modern technology and social media, we combine ubiquitous features such as dubious ratings, arbitrary target-setting, and an overblown sense of self-actualization into a ongoing project of eating dishes that represent 200 cuisines. The diverse cultural melting pot of New York City provides an ideal setting.

The rules, if you want to call them so, are simple. First, a cuisine must be catalogued (we call it the spreadsheet), second, be supplied by a reasonably authentic purveyor, and third, documented in some digital form. We also introduce the emoji scale.

On this GitHub page we attempt to give it an air of pseudo-rigor.

The project is separate but related to Jackson 42.
