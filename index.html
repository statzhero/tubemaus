About

TUBEMAUS are a duo formed in London and based in Long Island City. Their work explores the metaphorical meaning of “un-art”, where worldly themes are subdued to a primitive process for allegorical abstraction. Materials and sculptures typically form an installation reminiscent of arte povera. TUBEMAUS shies away from traditional labels and imbues an amateurish sense of self-reference. Originally trained as scientists, their art process is described as formulaic and parsimonious.
